Problems we solve

Most small businesses do not need a complicated AI platform. They need fewer missed messages, faster responses, and less repetitive admin.

✓

Missed calls and slow replies

AI receptionists and chat agents help capture customer intent even when staff are with another customer, out on a job, or after hours.

✓

Repeating the same answers

Common questions about services, availability, policies, and next steps can be handled consistently without pulling your team away from higher-value work.

✓

Leads that quietly go cold

Follow-up agents can qualify inquiries, remind customers, and move requests toward a booking instead of letting them sit in an inbox.

How implementation works

A lightweight build process designed around your existing business information and customer workflow.

1

Discovery Call

We map the questions, calls, and follow-ups that take the most time or get missed most often.

2

Business Info Collection

We gather FAQs, service details, scripts, policies, and routing rules so the agent reflects how your business actually works.

3

Agent Build

Your chatbot, receptionist, or follow-up agent is configured around your workflows, tone, and escalation rules.

4

Testing

We test common customer scenarios, edge cases, and handoff points before anything goes live.

5

Launch & Improve

After launch, we review real interactions and tune the agent so it stays useful as your business changes.

Most projects start with one focused agent, then expand once the workflow is proven.
