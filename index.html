Built for small teams that cannot answer everything manually.

Most small businesses do not need a complicated AI platform. They need fewer missed messages, faster responses, and less repetitive admin.

✓

Missed calls during busy hours

When your team is serving customers, on-site, or away from the desk, an AI receptionist can still capture the caller's reason and next step.

✓

Repeated questions eating up staff time

Customers can get clear answers about hours, pricing notes, services, policies, and availability without waiting for a manual reply.

✓

Leads going cold after no follow-up

Follow-up workflows can confirm details, remind customers, and keep promising inquiries from getting buried in your inbox.

✓

After-hours inquiries getting ignored

Chat and phone agents can collect useful information outside business hours so your team starts the next day with context.

✓

Manual booking or intake work

Agents can ask intake questions, collect preferences, and prepare clean handoffs before a team member takes over.

How VetraCore gets your agent running

A lightweight implementation process designed around your existing business information and real customer workflow.

1

Map the workflow

2

Build the agent

We create a chatbot, phone agent, or follow-up workflow using your real business information.

3

Test real scenarios

We test the agent against realistic customer questions and edge cases before launch.

4

Launch and refine

We deploy it, monitor results, and improve it based on real usage.
